Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Culinary Icon

Founded in 1999 by Chef Antonio Rossi, Savoria started as a small family kitchen with a big dream — to share the art of authentic cuisine with the world. What began as a 20-seat trattoria has blossomed into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: source the best ingredients, honor traditional cooking methods, and serve every guest with unparalleled hospitality. Every dish on our menu reflects decades of culinary expertise and a deep respect for the flavors that bring people together.

Today, Savoria stands as a beacon of gastronomic excellence, welcoming guests from around the globe to experience the magic that happens when passion meets the plate.
