Our Story

From a Small Firm to a Global Business Partner

Founded in 2009, AymanInfotech began as a boutique consulting firm with a handful of passionate professionals and a clear vision: to make enterprise-level business intelligence accessible to companies of all sizes.

Over the years, we've grown into a full-service business solutions provider, serving over 500 clients across 30+ countries. Our journey is marked by relentless innovation, deep client partnerships, and a commitment to delivering real, measurable outcomes.

Today, our team of 25+ specialists spans strategy, technology, marketing, and finance — working in perfect synergy to help our clients thrive in an ever-changing world.
