Our Story

Built on Excellence, Driven by Results

CorporaX was founded in 2012 with a singular mission: to help businesses of all sizes compete at the highest level. What started as a small strategy consultancy has grown into a global firm with offices in 5 countries and over 80 dedicated professionals.

We believe that every business, regardless of size or industry, deserves access to world-class expertise. That belief drives everything we do — from the strategies we craft to the solutions we build.

Today, CorporaX serves 500+ clients across 30+ countries, delivering measurable impact through an integrated approach that combines strategy, technology, design, and marketing.

Our Journey

A Decade of Impact & Growth

2012

Company Founded

CorporaX was founded in San Francisco with a team of 5 passionate strategists and a mission to democratize world-class business consulting.

2015

Tech Division Launch

Expanded into software development, launching our technology division and completing our first 50 client projects with a 100% satisfaction rate.

2018

Global Expansion
