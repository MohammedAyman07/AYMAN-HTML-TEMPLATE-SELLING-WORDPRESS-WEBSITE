FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can CorporaX start on my project?

We typically onboard new clients within 5–7 business days. For urgent projects, we offer an expedited onboarding process that can start within 48 hours of contract signing.

Do you work with startups or only established companies?

We work with businesses at every stage — from pre-seed startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our Starter plan is specifically designed to be accessible for early-stage companies.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first month, we'll provide a full refund — no questions asked.

Do you offer custom service packages?

Yes. If none of our standard plans fit your exact needs, contact us to discuss a custom package. We frequently build bespoke engagements for clients with unique requirements.
