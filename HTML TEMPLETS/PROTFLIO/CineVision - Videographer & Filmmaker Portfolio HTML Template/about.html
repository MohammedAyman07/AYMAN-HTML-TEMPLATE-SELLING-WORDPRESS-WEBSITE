My Story

Passion Meets Precision

Hi, I'm Alex Carter — a professional videographer, filmmaker, and visual storyteller based in New York City, available for projects worldwide.

My journey began with a borrowed camera and an obsession with capturing moments that made people feel something. Over 8 years, that obsession evolved into a career, collaborating with brands, couples, recording artists, and filmmakers across three continents.

I believe great video isn't just about technical excellence — it's about connecting with your audience on an emotional level. Every project I take on starts with one question: What story do we want to tell?
