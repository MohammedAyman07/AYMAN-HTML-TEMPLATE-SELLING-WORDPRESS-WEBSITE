FAQs

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Simple landing pages take 1–2 weeks. Multi-page websites typically 3–5 weeks. Full web applications depend on scope — usually 6–16 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline before any project begins.

What's your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% upfront deposit and 50% on delivery. For larger projects, I use milestone-based payments. I accept PayPal, Stripe, and bank transfers.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes! All projects come with 30 days of free support. After that, I offer maintenance packages starting at $99/month. I'm always available for urgent fixes.

Can you work with my existing team?

Absolutely. I have experience collaborating with in-house dev teams, designers, and project managers. I adapt to your workflow and tools (Slack, Jira, Notion, etc.).
