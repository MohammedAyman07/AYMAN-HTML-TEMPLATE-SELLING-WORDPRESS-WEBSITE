Lead Architect

Michael Anderson

Founder & Principal Designer

With a passion for minimalist design and sustainable architecture, Michael founded the studio in 2008. His vision was to create a practice that challenges the conventional boundaries of architecture, focusing instead on user experience and the harmonic integration of buildings within their environments.

Our work spans across various sectors including high-end residential, cultural institutions, and cutting-edge commercial spaces. We pride ourselves on a detail-oriented approach where every line has a purpose.

Our Design Philosophy
