My Story

My name is Alex Rivera, a self-taught digital artist and illustrator based remotely. I discovered my love for visual storytelling at a young age, sketching fantasy worlds in notebooks before transitioning to digital media.

Over the past 8 years, I've had the privilege of collaborating with game studios, book publishers, streaming platforms, and individual clients — producing artwork that spans character design, environment concept art, book cover illustrations, album artwork, and branded digital paintings.

My artistic style blends intricate detail with atmospheric color — heavily influenced by fantasy literature, mythology, and the ethereal beauty of nature. Every piece tells a story, and it's my mission to make that story unforgettable.
