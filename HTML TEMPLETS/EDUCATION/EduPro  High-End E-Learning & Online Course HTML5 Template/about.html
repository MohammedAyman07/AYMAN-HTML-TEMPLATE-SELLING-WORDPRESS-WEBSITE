Our Story

Who We Are & What We Stand For

EduPro was founded with a simple yet powerful belief: that every person on this planet deserves access to the best possible education. Since 2014, we have been building a platform where curiosity meets opportunity.

We partner with the world's leading universities, companies, and instructors to bring you cutting-edge courses that are relevant, practical, and career-focused. Our community of over 50,000 students proves that quality education can change lives.
