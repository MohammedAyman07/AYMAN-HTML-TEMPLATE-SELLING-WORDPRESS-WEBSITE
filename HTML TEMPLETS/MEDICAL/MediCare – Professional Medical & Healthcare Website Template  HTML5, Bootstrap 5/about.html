Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence in Healthcare

Founded in 2005, MediCare has grown from a small community clinic into a fully accredited multi-specialty hospital trusted by thousands of patients. Our journey has been driven by a singular purpose: to deliver compassionate, world-class medical care to every person who walks through our doors.

Today, MediCare is home to over 120 board-certified specialists, cutting-edge diagnostic facilities, and a 24/7 emergency department equipped to handle the most complex medical situations. We are ISO 9001 certified and proud recipients of numerous national healthcare awards.
